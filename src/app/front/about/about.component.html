HyperSense has experience developing a wide range of custom software solutions for web and mobile devices. We specialize in native mobile app development languages, including Node.js, Angular, SWIFT, and Kotlin.

We prefer to work under time and materials contracts. For certain projects, we also undertake fixed price contracts. We accept wire transfers in USD, EUR, GBP, or RON.

Yes! From day one, you retain access to all project assets, including design files, code, diagrams, and specs. You are the sole owner of IP rights for all project assets we create.
